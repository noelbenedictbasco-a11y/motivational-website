Introduction

Hi! I am Noel Benedict Basco, a student of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in Western Institute of Technology.

______________________________________________

Our entire lives we've been taught that we needed to be exeptional or remarkable, that average was something we needed to escape. That if we didn't do something remarkable with our lives, we failed.

But no one even stopped to define what average actually meant, a stable job, a partner, a house, two kids? Because if that's average, why were we ever taught to fear it?

The truth is none of those things were even the problem, everything you've been told about being an average has been a lie. Average was never about the life you live, it was always about something means deeper than that.
