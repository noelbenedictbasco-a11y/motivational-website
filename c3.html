Chapter 3

The Death

To reach fulfillment we have to go through this. You have to lose your ego, the version of yourself that needs centainly, that needs to look competent. That's the price of meaningful life, a small death of your identity.

Anything that brings you out of your comfort zone like going to a new coffee shop, talking to strangers, or go to some places without a plan. Because ego is what creates the comfort zone is where dreams mostly die.

So instead of risking discomfort now, ego offers you a trade. "Stay comfortable today, and deal with the consequences later". And those consequences don't show up immediately, They show up years later. It arrives as a sentence you hear one day..... and can't unhear

"IF ONLY I TRIED"

It's REGRET.

Noel Benedict Basco
