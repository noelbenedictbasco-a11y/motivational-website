Chapter 1

The Lie

What an average life actually is?

You can wake up every morning , go to school, come home, live quietly and still live a deeply fulfilled life. Millions of people dream of exactly that, Stability isn't enough.

Social media brainwashed us into believing that we should chase unrealistic perception of money and materialism and that is we didn't have this checklist of being done before a centain age (beetween 25-30), you're falling behind.

But an extra ordinary life isn't define by starting a 100 million pesos business, or becoming an athlete or celebrity. Because average was never about structure, it was never about 9-5 school schedule or a routine. It was always about your mentality.

A stable life isn't the problem. The problem was never challenging yourself within it. Because somewhere along the way, comfort quielty replaces courage. You stop trying new things, stop doing things you bad at. You only even choose the option you know will work out. And overtime you begin to resent the very life you once prayed for. And slowly days started to feel the same. They turn into weeks and months into years.

And one day you'll wake up and realized something terrifying. You never took a single risk. Never once step outside the routine. Never once ask more of yourself.

And thats what being Average really is.

Noel Benedict Basco
