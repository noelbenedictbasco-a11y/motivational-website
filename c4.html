Chapter 4

Escape

________________________________________________________________

The only way to live a life filled with regret is to never step outside your comfort zone. Many people think leaving the comfort zone means destroying their life or making extreme changes. But it doesn’t. It doesn’t mean quitting your job overnight to start a business or taking reckless risks. It simply means choosing growth over comfort, one small step at a time.

If you're wrong, you are willing to embarrass yourself, you are willing to try. You are willing to embrace discomfort.

Try something new. Pickup a new shell, talk to stranger, try a new retaurant, share your works before it's perfect or go somewhere unfamiliar without a plan. And this is how you need to live everyday.

IN THE PRESENT

Because this moment is all we have.

As humans, we tend to focus too much in the future. In a way it's paradox. Because we fear an average life so much, but only in hindsight. Because we fear discomfort in the present more.

So it was never about a 9-5 school schedule, a routine, or even comfort. Those were not the enemies. It was always letting those things----
